About Us

At CareNest, we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of underprivileged children and the elderly across India. Our mission is to provide food, shelter, education, and medical assistance to those in need, ensuring a brighter future for all. Through our various initiatives, we strive to bring hope and happiness to the less fortunate, empowering them with the resources and support they deserve. Join us in our journey to create a compassionate and caring society—because every act of kindness counts.
